6 Tips to remove the black crust on your pan

  1. 1. Salt

    Salt is one of the substances that is conveniently available in your kitchen. The method to remove the black crust in your pan or other cooking utensils is by heating the water in that pan and adding a bit of salt. Then, scrub it with the spatula while scraping the pan.
  2. 2. Bicarbonate Soda

    Bicarbonate of soda can be used together with lemon or vinegar. Heat the water in the pan until it boils and add some bicarbonate soda. Leave it at room temperature for a while before washing the pan.
  3. 3. Vinegar

    Boil vinegar in the pan or pot that has the black crust. Soak it for a while until the pot is really cool. Then, scrub as usual.
  4. 4. Lemon

    Lemon is also known as a good ingredient as a detergent. The method to use it for removing the black crust in your pan is easy. First, cut the lemon and boil it with its peel. Leave it to cool for 3-4 hours and wash the pot as usual.
  5. 5. Potato peel

    Put the potato peels in the pot and add water until it covers the black crust in the pot. Then, heat the water until it boils. The crust in the pot will come off by itself.
  6. 6. Freshly Dried Asam Keping

    Take a handful of freshly-dried asam keping and put it into the rusty pan/pot that you want to remove the crust. Let it boil for a while. Then, wait for it to cool and rub the surface of the pan or pot.

6 Fruits to prevent diabetes

  1. 1. Pears

    This fruit is enriched with high fibers that are good to regulate your blood sugar levels in the body. Besides, pears are also able to lower the level of harmful cholesterol in your body.
  2. 2. Apples

    Research from Harvard School of Public Health shows individual that take 5 apple in a weekhas much lower risk of getting diabetes.
  3. 3. Peach

    This fruit is good for an individual to prevent diabetes as well as for diabetes patients as it contains high level of natural fibers, vitamin C and also ia a source of antioxidant that good for the body.
  4. 4. Oranges

    Orange can help to slow down the absorption of excess sugar in the body. Besides, the Vitamin C content in it is also good enough to keep the heart functioning well.
  5. 5. Blueberries

    Based on the result of the study by the American Diabetes Association, the individual who practices the consumption of blueberries regularly has a 23 percent lower risk of suffering from diebetes.
  6. 6. Cherry

    Cherry fruit contains antioxidants known as anthocyanin and play the role as anti-inflammation as well as anti-aging. Consuming 10 cherries a day also helps stabilize blood sugar levels in your body.
