Buffalo Wings

Recipe:

Ingredients:

The beef

  1. 500 g beef or mutton mince
  2. 2 tbsp olive oil
  3. 1 finely diced large onion
  4. 1 finely diced large carrot
  5. 100 g garden peas
  6. 50 g sweetcorn
  7. 2 crushed garlic cloves
  8. 10 finely diced mushrooms
  9. 1 tbsp tomato pur√©e
  10. 2 dashes worcestershire sauce

The mash potato

  • 900 g peeled and diced potatoes
  • 85 g butter
  • 3 tbsp milk

The topping

  • 1/2 cup shredded cheese (cheddar, Colby, Gruyere, or Swiss)

Directions:

  1. Combine all ingredients (B) in a small bowl.
  2. Make sure all the chicken wings are dry.
  3. Place the chicken wings into a large bowl and sprinkle the flour mixture over the chicken wings, coating each wing evenly.
  4. Put the chicken wings in the refrigerator for 30 minutes (this helps the flour mixture to stick to the chicken wings).
  5. Heat oil, fry for 10-15 mins or until the chicken wings turn brown.
  6. Remove chicken wings from oil to a kitchen paper to drain.
  7. For the sauce, heat butter in a small saucepan over low heat until melted.
  8. Add in the rest of ingredients (C) and mix well. Remove from heat.
  9. Put the chicken wings into a large bowl, add sauce and stir.
  10. Coat all the chicken wings evenly.
