Resepi Che Nom:
Bubur Ayam McD and Garlic Oil

4.3 Stars      Preparation Time : 60 Minutes    Calorie : 148kcal

Ingredients :

  • Ingredients for chicken porridge:
    • 2 pieces of chicken filling
    • 3-4 slices of ginger
    • Scallion stems1-2 tablespoons of chicken stock
    • 4-5 cups of water
    • 1 cup of rice
    • A little salt
  • Ingredients for the topping:
    • 1-2 Red chillies
    • Onions
    • White pepper powder
    • Sesame oil
    • Fried onions
  • Ingredients for garlic oil:
    • 1 cup garlic
    • 1 Â½ cups of cooking oil

Instructions :

  • Ways to prep:
    1. Thinly slice ginger.
    2. Save the white base leeks for stew, thinly slice the green part for sprinkling.
    3. Red chili, remove the seeds and thinly slice.
  • Ways for fried onions:
    1. Thinly slice small shallots
    2. Mix red onion slices with salt and rice flour.
    3. Heat the oil and fry the onion. Stir until the onion is slightly browned.
    4. Remove and drain the oil.
  • Ways to make oil garlic:
    1. Finely chop the garlic
    2. Heat oil and add chopped garlic.
    3. Stir until the onion is almost brown.
    4. Transfer the garlic oil in a heat-resistant container and cool.
  • Ways to cook porridge:
    1. Boil the chicken, add ginger and spring onions.
    2. Add chicken stock (chicken stock cubes can be substituted) and water. Boil the chicken filling for approximately 10-15 minutes or until the chicken filling is cooked.
    3. Remove the chicken and other stew ingredients.
    4. Wash and drain the rice. Can be ground to be more crushed.
    5. Boil the rice in chicken stock water until the rice is fluffy and soft.
    6. Add a little salt, cover the fire and leave for 10-15 minutes. Can add water if dry.
    7. Shred the chicken.
    8. Serve porridge with chicken filling, red chili slices, fried onions, garlic oil, scallions, white pepper powder and a little sesame oil.

Resepi Khairul Aming:
Bread and Butter Pudding

4.0 Stars      Preparation Time : 60 Minutes    Calorie : 148kcal

Ingredients :

  • Ingredients for bread pudding:
    • 8 pieces of sandwich bread
    • 1 cup semi-sweet chocolate
    • 2 bananas
    • 2 cups of Milk Full Cream
    • 2 eggs
    • 1 tsp vanilla essence
    • 1/2 cup sugar
    • 2 tbsp butter
  • Ingredients for custard sauce:
    • 2 cups of Milk Full Cream
    • 2 tablespoon custard powder

Preparations :

  • Ways to make bread pudding:
    1. Firstly to prepare the mixture, add 2 cups of full cream milk into the pan.
    2. Add 2 eggs and 4 tablespoons of sugar.
    3. Then add 2 tablespoons of butter.
    4. Stir and cook for a while on the stove.
    5. Tear apart 8 slices of bread and place in a pan.
    6. Then put the banana slices. Alternate bread slices with banana slices until full.
    7. Pour the mixture over the shredded bread.
    8. Sprinkle semi-sweet chocolate.
    9. Bake in a temperature of 180 degrees Celsius for 50 minutes.
  • Ways to make custard sauce:
    1. Put 2 cups of full cream milk and 2 tablespoons of custard powder into a hot pan.
    2. Stir everything until thick.
    3. For serving, pour the custard sauce over the bread pudding.
