Shepherd's Pie Recipe

Ingredients

  • 1 1/2 lb ground beef or lamb
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tbsp tomato paste
  • 1 tbsp Worcestershire sauce
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1/2 tsp black pepper
  • 1/2 cup beef broth
  • 1 1/2 cups frozen mixed vegetables (peas, carrots, green beans, corn)
  • 2 lb potatoes, peeled and chopped
  • 1/2 cup milk
  • 1/4 cup butter
  • 1/2 cup grated cheddar cheese

Instructions

  1. Preheat the oven to 375Â°F.
  2. In a large skillet, brown the ground beef or lamb over medium-high heat. Drain the fat and add the onion and garlic. Cook for 2-3 minutes until the onion is softened.
  3. Add the tomato paste, Worcestershire sauce, salt, and black pepper. Stir well and cook for 1-2 minutes.
  4. Add the beef broth and mixed vegetables. Bring to a simmer and cook for 5-7 minutes until the vegetables are tender and the mixture is thickened.
  5. Meanwhile, place the chopped potatoes in a large pot and cover with cold water. Bring to a boil and cook for 15-20 minutes until the potatoes are tender.
  6. Drain the potatoes and return them to the pot. Add the milk and butter and mash until smooth.
  7. Spread the beef or lamb mixture in the bottom of a 9x13 inch baking dish. Top with the mashed potatoes, spreading them evenly over the meat mixture.
  8. Sprinkle the grated cheddar cheese over the potatoes.
  9. Bake for 25-30 minutes until the cheese is melted and the pie is heated through.
